Infosys Narayana Murthy: A Life of Achievement

Achievements

1981

Co-founds Infosys

1997

Infosys becomes the first Indian company to be listed on NASDAQ

2000

Infosys becomes the first Indian company to be ranked among the top 500 companies in the world by Fortune magazine

2004

Murthy is awarded the Padma Vibhushan, India's second-highest civilian award

2014

Murthy is awarded the Infosys Lifetime Achievement Award
